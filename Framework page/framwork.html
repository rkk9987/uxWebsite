Our Framework
Framework

We start with customer-centric digital product development approach with our expertise needed to make your product standout in the digital market. we are flexible to support you at every stage in the product development process.

Discover

We gain an empathetic understanding of the Idea and problem you’re trying to solve, typically through Market/user research. Empathy is crucial to a human-centered products.

Design

It is an experimental phase, where we aim is to identify the best possible solution for each problem/Ideas found and we rigorously test them with users so that we can return to previous stages to make further iterations, alterations and refinements to discover or rule out alternative solutions.

Define

We analyze your observations and synthesize them to define the core problems that we have identified.

Build

We starts building product on final design iteration and makes it funtional adding world class experience.

Testing

Our primary goal of testing is to identify all the defects and errors in the software before the implementation phase. and we want to maintain the quality and gain your client’s trust and confidence.

Our PDLC
Product development Lifecycle

UX-Prodx’s product development cycle is the process of taking a your product from an idea through its market release and beyond.

Idea Discussion

We brainstorm with our clients and our product experts look for ways to solve problems for your target user or user personas, and during this phase, our team will generate several product ideas.

Idea Validation

After the first phase, our product team will have a long list of product design concepts with a goal now to narrow the list to one product design idea or feature worth pursuing. we do practice several ways of screening ideas to learn which are the most viable.

Prototyping

Our product design creates a very simple mockup of the application and test it with a focus group or small group of customers for their feedback.

Product Development

After gathering users' feedback on prototypes or mockups, our product development team starts to build a minimum viable product (MVP).

Quality Assurance

Our QA team ensures software usability by running tests and preparing reports on the effectiveness and defects to the development team, whether the actual software product matches expected outcomes.

Product Launch

After product development and testing MVP, client is now ready to launch it to the public. As Product MVP will help the client to gain several important insights at once.
